//General moving commands
"a" = Moves device till EXT_INT_1 is high, then it sets that POS as 0 inside the motor driver.
"b" = Moves device left @ speed of 1 till EXT_INT_1 is low.
"c" = Moves device right @ speed of 1 till EXT_INT_1 is low.
"d" = Moves device left @ speed of 1 till EXT_INT_2 is high.
"e" = Moves device right @ speed of 1 till EXT_INT_3 is high.
"x" = Show firmware information
"z" = Software reset device

//Deprecated
"f" = Rotate right @ speed of 5.
